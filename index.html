About This Project

Millions of Americans face rising healthcare costs — a 3-day hospital stay now averages $30,000, nearly 700% more than in 2000. For uninsured individuals, especially in cities like Los Angeles with large homeless populations, even basic diagnosis can be financially devastating.

MediScan AI is a hybrid AI diagnostic tool built from scratch to make preliminary disease identification more accessible. It uses a Naive Bayes retrieval system combined with a fine-tuned DistilBERT reranker — trained on ~7,000 disease-symptom pairs sourced from Mayo Clinic and MedlinePlus — to rank likely diagnoses from plain-text symptom descriptions.

How It Works

  1. 1You describe your symptoms in plain text
  2. 2An LLM extracts structured symptoms from your input
  3. 3Naive Bayes retrieval filters the top 20 candidate diseases
  4. 4DistilBERT reranker reorders candidates by learned patterns
  5. 5Results are returned ranked by confidence
